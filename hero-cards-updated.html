Card Tier Specifications

Hero Card

  • Availability: Every registered player
  • Purpose: Individual player profile and stats
  • Updates: Stats auto-update after each match
  • Physical Drop: Monthly edition with updated stats
  • Digital Access: Instant via app after registration
  • QR Code: Links to registration + viral loop
  • Color Scheme: Electric Pink/Purple gradient

Game Card

  • Unlock Requirement: Participate in any organized match
  • Purpose: Collectible memory of specific games played
  • Flexibility: Play with anyone, anywhere - still get rewarded
  • Physical Drop: Monthly batch includes all games from that period
  • Digital Access: Instant after match organizer reports results
  • Card Details: Game name, date, location, score, your role & stats
  • Color Scheme: Cyan/Purple gradient

Trophy Card

  • Unlock Requirement: Win city league championship
  • Rarity: Limited holographic edition
  • Purpose: Elite status symbol and bragging rights
  • Physical Drop: Special holographic finish
  • Digital Access: Permanent achievement badge
  • Scarcity: Only 1 champion per city/season
  • Color Scheme: Gold/Orange with holographic overlay
